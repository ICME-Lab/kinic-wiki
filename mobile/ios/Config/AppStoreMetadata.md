## Short Description

Capture browser links into Kinic Wiki from the iOS share sheet.

## Description

KinicWikiApp lets Kinic Wiki users send URLs from Safari and other iOS apps into a selected writable Kinic Wiki database. Shared URLs are queued through the Share Extension, then submitted after Internet Identity sign-in and database selection.

## Privacy Notes

- Uses Internet Identity for sign-in.
- Stores pending shared URLs and selected database ID in the app group container.
- Does not declare tracking.
- Does not collect analytics in the native app.

## Review Notes

Sign in with Internet Identity, select a writable database, then share an HTTP or HTTPS URL into "Save to KinicWiki".
